TradeScanner brings player-to-player commerce back to life when the Auction House isn't available.

Monitor guild chat, trade channels, and guild confederations to discover live WTS/WTB offers, craft requests, and trading opportunities. Whether you're playing on a Fresh server, a guild-economy challenge, or a private server, TradeScanner helps buyers and crafters find each other.

**Smart Craft Detection**

Guild Economy ships with a built-in database of over 1,100 craftable items and 130+ enchants across 10 professions. Open your profession windows to automatically learn your character's recipes and unlock personalized alerts.

When someone posts a request for an item you can craft:

• Gold-highlighted matching offers • Sound alerts and minimap notifications • Dedicated "Sellable" tab with real-time counters • One-click whisper to contact the buyer

**Trade Network & Guild Confederations**

Supports GreenWall and guild confederations.

Craft orders can be synchronized across connected guilds, allowing crafters and customers to find each other even across multiple guild communities. Trade offers remain local to your guild while crafting requests can be shared federation-wide.

**Order Validation & Delivery Tracking**

Once a craft order is accepted, a **Validate** button appears for the crafter and the buyer. Click it when the goods change hands and the order is removed for everyone in the network — no more stale orders lingering on every member's panel.

Even better, validation can be automatic: if the buyer also runs the addon, **completing the trade auto-validates the order**. Hand over the full amount and the order disappears; hand over part of it and the **remaining quantity updates everywhere** so everyone sees exactly what's left to deliver. Buyers without the addon are covered too — the crafter simply clicks Validate.

**Sell Directly From Your Bags**

Need to sell something?

Simply Alt + Right-Click an item in your bags to create a WTS listing. Set quantity, price, and notes through a simple popup. Listings are automatically shared with other TradeScanner users.

**Organized Trading Interface**

Browse opportunities through five dedicated tabs:

• All • Sales (WTS) • Wanted (WTB) • Sellable (requests you can fulfill) • Orders (craft requests)

Smart deduplication keeps the list clean by tracking a single active offer per player and item.

**Additional Features**

• Guild chat and custom trade channel monitoring • English and French keyword support (WTS, WTB, VDS, ACH, CHERCHE, B>, and more) • International price parsing (1.5g, 1,5g, 1.50g) • Guild profession directory (/ts profs) • Persistent message history and debug tools • Trade-window order validation with partial-delivery tracking • Addon-to-addon synchronization network • Fully configurable channels, keywords, and notifications

**Commands**

Use /ts help to see the complete command list.

Popular commands:

• /ts profs — guild profession registry • /ts confed — confederation management • /ts help — command reference

**Perfect for:**

• Season of Discovery Fresh realms • Guild economy challenges • Auction House restricted servers • Community-driven economies
